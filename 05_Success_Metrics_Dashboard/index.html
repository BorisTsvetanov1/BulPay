Post-commit Payment Success Rate
i Share of submitted sends (after PIN) where the payment completed successfully. Early drop-offs before confirm are excluded. A low rate means something failed after the user committed—not only banks or processors (declines, outages), but also connectivity, timeouts, or issues in BulPay (bugs, configuration, retries, reconciliation). Error handling is how reliably you detect, recover, and explain those failures; use decline codes and internal error classes to see where losses come from. Bands: ≥98.5% good · 97–98.5% caution · 95.5–97% poor.
