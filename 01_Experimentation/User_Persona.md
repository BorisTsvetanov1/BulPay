---

## 📖 Background

George is a lower-class working man and a father of two. He is not tech savvy but can get around a mobile app. He values simplicity over bloated apps with numerous features. His schedule is often full, so he needs a quick and relaxed way to send money to friends and family members, without thinking twice.

---

## 🎯 Goals

- Send money quickly and easily with no stress *(e.g. for food and tuition for his kids, for sharing bills with spouse and siblings, for rent to his landlord)*.
- Save as much money as possible by avoiding local banks' high fees.
- Save time by using seamless online payments *(no app freezes, no in-cash payments)*.

---

## 😤 Pain Points

- Local bank fees are too high for him.
- Local banks' mobile app UI is confusing and online payments often crash and freeze, causing him much frustration.
- Is annoyed by all the passwords and PIN codes that he needs to remember across multiple apps.
- Does not comfortably trust online banks *(e.g. Revolut)* due to lack of Bulgarian customer support and lack of physical Bulgarian locations.

---

## 🔄 Current Behaviours

- Sends money weekly to several family members.
- Needs to pay for his bills *(water/gas/electricity)* via EasyPay. Prefers to do it with cash to avoid EasyPay's fees for online transfers.
- Checks account balances every other day out of anxiety or lack of trust.

---

## 📱 Tech Profile

- Primarily uses an **Android** phone.
- Comfortable with downloading new apps but needs them to be **intuitive**.
- Uses **Revolut's** digital wallet for small payments.
- Uses a **local bank app** for bigger transactions, savings, and investments.
- Lacks information about how online banks work *(e.g. was not aware that his salary can go through Revolut)*.
