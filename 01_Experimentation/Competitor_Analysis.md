---
Insights: All competitors offer robust digital banking, but none is tailored to older, non-tech-savvy Bulgarians. Traditional banks (UBB, DSK) have local trust, but demand high fees while their mobile services are not intuitive or simplified for novices. Modern wallets (Revolut, iCard) offer rich features at low costs, yet primarily attract younger users and lack in-person support. This suggests BulPay could seize an opportunity by combining the trust and support of a local bank with the convenience and low cost of a digital wallet – targeting the 45~65 demographic’s need for simple, stress-free, low-cost mobile payments.

>
> - SEPA Instant (SCT Inst) becomes mandatory for all Bulgarian PSPs on **January 1, 2027** per EU Instant Payments Regulation.
> - BGN was retired on **January 1, 2026** upon Bulgaria's Eurozone accession.
> - App ratings as of April 2026 — Google Play / App Store.
